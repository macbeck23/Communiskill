Accessibiity

At CommuniSkill, we are committed to providing an inclusive and accessible experience for all users.

We understand the importance of accessibility and have implemented several features to ensure our platform is usable by individuals with diverse abilities.

Below are the accessibility options available to enhance your browsing experience:

1. High Contrast Mode:
Enable high contrast mode for better visibility.
This feature enhances the contrast between text and background, making content more readable for users with visual impairments.

2. Font Customisation:
Adjust font size, style, and spacing to suit your reading preferences.
Our platform allows you to customise text settings for a more comfortable reading experience.

3. Keyboard Shortcuts:
Navigate through the website using keyboard shortcuts.
This feature simplifies interaction for users who may have difficulty using a mouse or touchpad.

4. Screen Reader Compatibility:
CommuniSkill is compatible with screen reader software, enabling users with visual impairments to access and understand website content seamlessly.

5. Alternative Text for Images:
All images on our platform are equipped with descriptive alternative text.
This ensures that users relying on screen readers receive meaningful information about the images displayed.

6. Simplified Layout:
Opt for a simplified layout to reduce visual clutter.
This option provides a streamlined interface, focusing on essential content elements for a distraction-free experience.

7. Video Transcripts and Captions:
Videos on CommuniSkill are accompanied by transcripts and captions.
Transcripts offer a textual version of the video content, while captions provide synchronized text to enhance understanding for users with hearing impairments.

8. Language Options:
Our platform supports multiple languages, allowing users to select their preferred language for a more comfortable browsing experience.

Feedback and Assistance:
We value your feedback regarding our accessibility features. If you encounter any challenges or have suggestions for improvement, please contact our support team at accessibility@communiskill.com. Your input helps us enhance our accessibility options and provide an inclusive environment for all users.

At CommuniSkill, we are dedicated to making our platform accessible to everyone. Your experience matters, and we are continually working to create a more inclusive digital space for our diverse community.
